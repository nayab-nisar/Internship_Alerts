Why people choose us


It’s simple. We Serve Best Food.

We're proud to serve the best Mediterranean cuisine, offering homemade dishes from Syria, Lebanon, and Iraq. Whether you're in the mood for sizzling Iraqi kabab, a hearty Syrian breakfast, or Lebanese tabbouleh, our menu has something for everyone. A highlight is our hearty Syrian breakfast with dishes like fatteh hummus loaded with almonds, pomegranate seeds, chickpeas, and crisp shards of pita bread. Whether you prefer meat or are vegetarian, our breakfast menu has options for you. Our menu goes beyond breakfast, featuring a variety of kebab plates like lamb, chicken, and lamb/beef skewers, all grilled to perfection over charcoal.

Our Journey


A Journey of Culture and Flavor

At Ottawa Kabab, we are passionate about sharing the authentic tastes of the Middle East with our customers. Our story begins with our owner, Mamown Srdah, who grew up in Syria and has always had a deep love for the rich and complex flavors of his culture’s cuisine. In late 2021, Srdah took over Ottawa Kabab, which had previously been an Iraqi-owned restaurant. With his extensive knowledge of Syrian cuisine, Srdah set out to create a menu that showcased the best of his country’s dishes while also featuring Lebanese and Iraqi favorites. From our sizzling Iraqi kabab to our hand-crafted middle eastern appitizers, each dish is carefully crafted using traditional recipes passed down through generations.

What types of cuisine does Ottawa Kabab serve?
▼
Ottawa Kabab serves Middle Eastern cuisine, with a focus on Syrian dishes. Our menu features a range of options for both meat and vegetarian eaters, including appetizers, dips, salads, entrees, and desserts.
Does Ottawa Kabab offer takeout and delivery?
▼
Yes, Ottawa Kabab offers both takeout and delivery options for our customers’ convenience. To place an order for takeout, simply give us a call, and we will have your meal ready for you to pick up. If you prefer delivery, you can order through our website or third-party delivery platforms such as UberEats or SkipTheDishes.
Does Ottawa Kabab have vegetarian options?
▼
Yes, we have a range of vegetarian options on our menu, including dips such as hummus, baba ganoush, and muhammara, as well as entrees such as the vegetarian platter, which includes falafel, grape leaves, and tabbouleh. We also have salads and side dishes that are vegetarian-friendly
